- **Location**: Siili Solutions, Helsinki  
- **Date**: 30.9.2023
- **Organizers**: 5
- **Attendees**: 45
- **Keyboards**: ~140ish

There had been many requests for hosting a Helsinki meetup in 2023, but we didn’t have a proper location for the meetup until Siili Solutions were kind enough to let us use their office. This meetup differed from previous meetups in the way that there were no outside sponsorships, and the meetup was purely focused on freely roaming around and trying out different boards. We received really good feedback about the meetup, and some even said that this was the favorite meetup (so far)!

Siili Solutions, you are awesome ❤️
